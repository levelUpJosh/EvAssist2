Charging

Upon clicking into this section, you'll notice that a map appears with some markers.

Each marker represents a charging station contained in the Firebase realtime database and the app will update in realtime based on which devices press the button to say they're at the site.

Tap on any desired location to see its information.

You'll then be given information from the realtime database about the site. Until you leave this page, you'll receive notifications about the site's availability.
